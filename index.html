What Is
Beat Club ?

Beat Club is unlike any language-focused social group you've ever encountered. With a core rule that insists on communicating exclusively in English and forbids the use of Malayalam, it's a place where linguistic adventure thrives. Instead of traditional language learning, the club offers a unique and engaging environment that challenges its members to elevate their English skills while having a blast. Picture this: the consequences for speaking Malayalam include an electrifying and theatrical response from other club members, which not only adds an element of excitement but also ensures you'll never forget the importance of sticking to the rule. It's an English-speaking journey filled with camaraderie, memorable interactions, and an opportunity to boost your language skills in a thrilling and welcoming atmosphere. Join Beat Club, and embark on a language adventure like no other!
